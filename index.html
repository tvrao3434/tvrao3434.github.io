About Me

I am a professional Production Designer and Art Director with over 28 years of extensive experience in the Indian film industry. My creative journey has evolved into leading art direction teams for numerous high-profile productions, including my work as Art Director for the film 'Takshakudu' (2025). I specialize in conceptualizing immersive environments, detailed 3D modeling, and realistic set designs. My vision is to blend technical mastery with artistic storytelling to bring cinematic worlds to life.
